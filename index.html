About Melissa

Melissa is an Atlanta-area native, having grown up in Marietta, GA. She attended Vanderbilt University in Nashville, TN where she was a Chancellor's Scholar and double-majored in Human and Organizational Development and Spanish. After graduating, Melissa completed a two year term of service in AmeriCorps where she worked with children in Atlanta Public Schools. She continued her career in education for a few more years before making the switch to real estate in the spring of 2016.

She lives in Grant Park with her husband Michael, their son Oliver, and their two little dogs, Kiki and Peanut.
